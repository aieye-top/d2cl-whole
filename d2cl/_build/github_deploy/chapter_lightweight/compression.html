3.7. compression¶

3.7.1. 针对生成模型的协同进化压缩算法(ICCV2019)¶

在CycleGAN中的两个生成器网络将会被同时压缩：

(3.7.1)¶\[\begin{split}\begin{aligned} \hat{G}_{1}, \hat{G}_{2} &=\arg \min _{G_{1}, G_{2}} \mathcal{N}\left(G_{1}\right)+\mathcal{N}\left(G_{2}\right) \\ &+\gamma\left(\mathcal{L}_{\text {DisA}}\left(G_{1}, D_{1}\right)+\lambda \mathcal{L}_{\text {cyc }}\left(G_{1}, G_{2}, X\right)\right) \\ \quad+& \gamma\left(\mathcal{L}_{\text {DisA }}\left(G_{2}, D_{2}\right)+\lambda \mathcal{L}_{\text {cyc }}\left(G_{2}, G_{1}, Y\right)\right) \end{aligned}\end{split}\]
